A J-45 with Rosewood Back and Sides:

Step up your tone with the rosewood back and sides Gibson Acoustic J-45 Custom acoustic-electric guitar. A classic, this round-shoulder, dreadnought acoustic guitar is known for its warm, balanced tone and projection. The J-45 Custom features all that rich tone, thanks to its AA-grade Sitka spruce top and rosewood body. You'll love the acoustic plugged-in sounds of this guitar thanks to its LR Baggs VTC pickup system. Enhance the sound of your live and studio acoustic performances with the Gibson J-45 Custom.

Gibson J-45 Custom Acoustic-electric Guitar at a Glance: "The Workhorse" features choice tonewoods for incredible tone Rosewood Fingerboard with Rolled Edges and Dot Inlays Get amazing sound onstage courtesy of LR Baggs VTC electronics "The Workhorse" features choice tonewoods for incredible tone Known as "The Workhorse," the Gibson J-45 body style is among Gibson's most beloved. This particular workhorse sits at the top of Gibson's J-45 line, and features a superb blend of tonewoods, giving you rich, strong sound - day-in, day-out. The J-45 Custom features a AA-grade Sitka spruce top for presence, while premium rosewood adorns its back and sides. Other touches include hand-scalloped, radiused top bracing, herringbone binding, a tapered dovetail neck joint, and a double-ring rosette filled with abalone.

Rosewood Fingerboard with Rolled Edges and Dot Inlays The Gibson J-45 Custom's fingerboard comprises high-grade rosewood, which is personally inspected and qualified by Gibson experts before it enters the Gibson factories. It's a resilient and durable wood, making way for a balanced and stable fingerboard, and giving each chord and note enhanced clarity and bite. The dot inlays on the J-45 are made of genuine mother-of-pearl-of-pearl, and are added to the fingerboard using a special Gibson process that eliminates gaps and doesn't require fillers. What's more, the J-45 Custom's fingerboard boasts a rolled edge that's lightly beveled, which enhances the instrument's smooth playability.

Get amazing sound onstage courtesy of LR Baggs VTC electronics The Gibson Acoustic J-45 Custom comes with LR Baggs's popular VTC acoustic pickup system, a popular choice among Sweetwater's acoustic guitarists. This system features a low-profile under-saddle transducer that's proven more stable and durable than many standard ribbon-type pickups. Its sleek and effective design removes unnecessary internal components to enhance the coupling between the pickup and the guitar and improve the sensitivity of the transducer for a robust, lively, and natural acoustic sound with excellent dynamics and delicate high end. Combined with an onboard preamplifier with an unobtrusive and removable volume control, mounted just inside the soundhole, the VTC gives your Gibson Acoustic J-45 Custom the amplification you need, with zero compromises for tone or convenience.
